Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to the following types of leave:

1. Annual Leave: 20 days per year
2. Sick Leave: 10 days per year
3. Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
4. Paternity Leave: 2 weeks

Unused leave can be carried forward for up to 1 year.
All leave must be approved by your manager in advance.

For emergencies, inform your team lead at the earliest.

Policy last updated: Jan 2024
